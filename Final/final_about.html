A Little Bit About Me!

Born and raised in the Hot Springs area. I got interested in drawing from japanese comicbooks. They inspired me to illustrate visuals and I always wanted to capture the dynamic imagery that was captured in their pages.

From my 4th grade year until now, I have been drawing illustrations in my free time. Art is something I definitely will always have as a part of my life. I have worked with a myriad of medias and programs over the years. I've gained a lot of experience with the adobe suite to do graphic designs for various projects.

For the majority of my art journey, I have worked with ink creating black and white illustrations. Recently I've been working with watercolor paints and trying to improve my coloring skills. I've really love working with the paints, and watercolors have always had an aestetic that I really love.

Moving forward, I would like to do chracter designs and cover art for books if possible. I've been doing a few portraits on occasion and have had some people express interests in that in the past. As far as character design, I have created character art for my friends who I play Dungeons & Dragons with. I think it would be cool to work on chracter art for people's D&D characters and help bring their visions to life.
